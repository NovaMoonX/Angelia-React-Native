Beta update notes

Last updated: 2026-05-11
Branch: copilot/fix-invite-button-state

Use this file as the source for the next beta update modal. Keep it short, warm, and focused on what changed for beta testers.

Current branch highlights:
- Circle invites now stay in sync right away, so invite buttons and pending state feel more reliable.
- The feed notification badge now reflects pending circle invites in addition to join and connection requests.
- The feed and account screens have a clearer “How Angelia works” entry point.
- Status updates can now stay active until you clear them, which makes the new status picker feel more natural.
- Daily Circle invite flow is cleaner, with invite, share, and QR actions grouped more clearly.
- The feed now stays focused on other people’s posts, while your own post engagement moved to a dedicated activity view.
- There is a new post activity icon in the feed header that opens “Your Post Activity,” where your posts are grouped with reaction, private-note, and message counts.
- Your post activity screen includes Circle filtering so it is easier to spot which posts are getting attention.
- Feed circle filtering now excludes your own circles entirely, and the scope picker has been removed to keep the filter focused on other people’s circles.
- Post activity now uses the same post cards as feed, and its Circle chips include all of your circles (even before a circle has posts).
- Post activity now follows the post-detail indicator style: reactions are shown on the card itself, while new private notes/messages use highlighted action pills with unread indicators.
- Post activity is now simplified to one clear label per post: “New activity since last seen.”
- Post activity now calls out which reaction emojis are new, and uses smarter seen logic: reaction-only updates clear when the card is viewed, while notes/messages clear when opening that post.
- Camera: can now zoom
