Current branch highlights (1.0.10):

- Reacting and joining a conversation in quick succession no longer wipes your reaction or flashes the text input away.
- Replying to a reply (depth 2) stays connected to the original thread with a clearer quote of the root message.
- QR codes and shared invite links route to the same public invite screen for a consistent experience.
