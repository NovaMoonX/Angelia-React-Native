Use this file as the source for the next beta update modal. Keep it short, warm, and focused on what changed for beta testers.

Current branch highlights:
- Private notes now show up right away when you're viewing your post — no more reloading the app to see a note someone just sent you.
- Tap any private note to open a back-and-forth conversation with the other person, just like post conversations.
- Post details now highlights when someone replied to a private note, with a chevron showing notes are tappable.
- New reactions on your posts stay unread until you open post detail — scrolling past cards in Post Activity no longer clears them.
- Feed and Post Activity now have expiring-soon filters so you can spot posts about to expire; on Feed, a separate row below status filters highlights ones you haven't reacted to yet.
- Notifications groups activity by post (replies, new posts, and more) so you can see what happened on each post at a glance.
- Post creation tips stay dismissed once you close them — they won't pop back up when you start a new post.
- Notifications for deleted posts and circles are cleaned up automatically.
- Circle post notification settings now list every connected person's Daily Circle, with search and collapsed circle cards to keep things tidy.
- Notifications inbox items now show more at-a-glance context — post snippets, priority tier badges, and circle labels — so you know what happened before you tap.
- Notifications splits inbox into **Activity on your posts** and **Activity**, with less duplicate info on each card and a per-section **Mark all seen** button.
