FULL DESCRIPTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Group chats are chaos. Social media is exhausting. And somehow, staying
genuinely connected to the people you love has never felt harder.

Angelia is a private sharing app built for families and close-knit groups. It
gives everyone their own space to share life's moments — organized, intentional,
and without the noise.

Here's how it works:

  • Daily Circles — Every person gets their own Daily Circle, a personal space
    to share everyday updates. Think of it like a private mini-feed, just for
    the people in your life.

  • Custom Circles — Create private circles around any topic: kids' milestones,
    travel adventures, recipes, workout goals — whatever brings your people
    together. You choose who's in.

  • Subscribe to what matters — You don't have to follow everything. Subscribe
    only to the circles you actually care about. No more drowning in updates
    that don't apply to you.

  • Updates that fade naturally — Posts automatically disappear after six months.
    That means you can share freely without building a permanent record. Recent
    moments stay front and center; old ones fade away — just like real memory.

  • Private messaging — Have a quick one-on-one conversation directly in the
    app, no need to switch to another platform.

  • No algorithm, no ads, no performance pressure — Angelia doesn't push viral
    content or reward engagement metrics. It's just the people you chose,
    sharing what they want, when they want.

Angelia is for the long-distance family that wants to feel closer. The busy
parent who wants to share without keeping a highlight reel. The tight-knit
friend group that's tired of chaotic group threads.

Connection governed by logic, not algorithms.
